black forest tort

Cake:

make a dark chocolate cake in three 9" pans. bake 25 min at 350.

Filling:

  1. 1 1/2 bars (total 6 oz.) bakers German sweet chocolate - Melt in microwave, stir until smooth and then let cool.

  2. 1 1/2 C. SOFT TYPE margarine - when chocolate is cool, blend in margarine and mix thoroughly.

  3. 1/2 c. 2.25 oz chopped almonds - stir into chocolate mix

    4. when cake is cool, spread half the filling between each of the layers making one three layer cake.

    Frosting:

    • 1 pint whipping cream for the top and sides.
    • or
    • 1/2 pint whipping cream for the just the top.
    add 1 T. and 1 t. and whip cream until stiff. Very close to serving time, frost cake with whipping cream. with a vegetable peeller, make chocolate with the remaining unmelted chocolateand sprinkle over top of cake.

Enjoy!
